The Man Behind
Kira

Born to a family of law enforcement, Light Yagami was Japan's brightest student — top of his class at To-Oh University, a national tennis champion, and a prodigy feared even by his own professors. He was, by every visible measure, perfect.

Then the Death Note fell from the sky. And perfection found its purpose.

Beneath the model-student facade lived a mind that had already grown bored of a world too corrupt to fix through ordinary means. Light didn't break — he decided. And in that decision, a god was born.
